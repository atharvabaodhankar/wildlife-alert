Reporting Tips

Take Clear Photos

Capture clear, well-lit photos from multiple angles if possible. Photos help with species identification.

Be Specific with Location

Include landmarks, GPS coordinates, or detailed location descriptions to help researchers.

Report Quickly

Submit your sighting as soon as possible while details are fresh in your memory.

Safety First

Never approach wild animals. Observe from a safe distance and respect their natural behavior.
